WeatheryApp

The WeatheryApp is web application that has been developed for you at our company in collaboration with Database Analyst Manisha Pandey , Hardware Manager Gaurav Ghule , Web Designer Shreyash Kshirsagar & Resource Manager Naima Farhat. With the help of our collaborators, Weatheryapp is the world's largest Weather Site with millions of users which uses our services everyday.

At our company, we focus on weather prediction and meteorological data visualisation. We have a wealth of experience in presenting weather data and so we decided to take advantage of our knowledge and experience to create a top-quality application that clearly displays meteorological data from around the world and allows you to monitor weather development for any place on earth.
